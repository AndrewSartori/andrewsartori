About Me

From Product Manager to Web Developer

M.E.R.N. Stack Developer & P.M.

My name is Andrew Sartori and I am a junior web developer! I have 5+ years of product management experience with responsibilities spanning the gamut from inbound to outbound product management. I have built and managed products in the digital health industry as well as the eLearning industry. In 2013, I started a company at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) where I managed a team of engineers and clinicians to build a suite of tablet-based home healthcare forms, a Google Glass based clinical documentation tool, and a mobile application that uses the camera on an Android device to visualize blood-flow to any area of tissue.
